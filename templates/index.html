How does Verve WORK?

#1

Create a Verve account!

Your Verve account is your small business identity. It will have all the details your buyers will see, trust & shop from directly! It encapsulates your brand identity, reflecting reliability and fostering a sense of trust that encourages seamless transactions and a positive shopping experience.

#2

Add your products to showcase your offerings.

Enhance your Verve profile by adding a diverse range of products or services, providing your customers with a comprehensive view of what your business has to offer. Utilize appealing visuals and detailed descriptions to capture their attention and boost engagement.

#3

Receive buying requests and connect with potential buyers.

As customers express interest in your products, respond promptly to buying requests. Engage in conversations to understand their needs and provide personalized assistance, building a rapport that encourages successful transactions.

#4

List and sell your products seamlessly on Verve.

Once you receive buying requests, list your products for sale on Verve. Provide clear product details, negotiate terms, and finalize transactions securely. Enjoy the simplicity of selling your thrifted items directly through your Verve link.
